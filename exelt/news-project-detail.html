The “Levelling up” project

John Clow
Sept, 14 2020
5 views
the “Levelling up” project. The project is designed to enable schools and regions to achieve high quality language teaching despite having minimal resources.
The project is based on Exelt’s research into Minimal Resource Teaching (MRT) and its work to combine the Exelt app with a range of available resources to achieve results whatever the situation.
KOBFA is now providing free services in selected schools around Thailand, experimenting with the resources available, combining everyday objects with the Exelt app and developing teaching techniques to ensure that every Thai student gets access to the best teaching possible.
