EAP (English for Academic Purposes)
This course is designed to help teachers drive their students’ academic success in key English exams, including IELTS, PET, KET, and TOEIC. Educators will learn effective strategies to simplify exam formats, teach practical techniques, and build essential academic skills that ensure strong performance.
The course also demonstrates how making exam preparation fun and engaging can motivate students to actively participate, boosting their confidence and enhancing learning outcomes. Our integrated app further supports academic achievement by offering students flexible self-study options to practice and refine their skills at their own pace.
Tailored to the needs and experience levels of teachers, this course is very flexible, and can be delivered as a standalone program or as a complement to our existing exam preparation courses.
Learning Outcomes
By the end of this course, participants will be able to:
1. Understand the formats and requirements of IELTS, PET, KET, and TOEIC exams and teach strategies for success effectively.
2. Deliver targeted practice in key skills such as reading comprehension, listening accuracy, academic writing, and speaking fluency.
3. Teach practical techniques for managing time, handling different question types, and avoiding common exam pitfalls.
4. Design engaging and interactive lessons that keep students motivated and build their confidence for exams.
5. Integrate self-study tools to help students strengthen their skills independently and develop autonomous learning habits.
