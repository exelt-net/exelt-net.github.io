At KOBFA we can design courses to meet the language learning needs of any company or organization.

What we’ve found out...

Times are hard and competition is fierce. We need to show a high level of professionalism in everything we do.

It will save us money if we can develop and promote from within. Our staff need better English, but we don’t have the time or money to send them on training courses or long study courses.
-GM Ibis Hotel

Tailored courses

At KOBFA we design tailored courses for organizations and because of this we can not only help
staff with their English but also combine this with training in the exact information and methods the organization wishes them to follow.

Learning while training

By blending language learning with staff training, and maximizing the ability to train and promote from within, we can help organizations make significant savings on staff development while preparing them for any role or situation in an English speaking environment.

Make free time worth

In addition, our software allows staff to study on their own devices anywhere, meaning that they can continue to develop in their free time and even in downtime on the job they can be using their time productively.

Anytime, Anywhere

Our consultants are available to supplement self-study with face to face or online sessions, all done at a time that suits you and your staff. This self-study aspects allows us to minimize the time needed with our consultants, thereby saving you time and money!
