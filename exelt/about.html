We combine our understanding of Thai culture with global experience of the ELT industry.

Advance and Understand

At KOBFA our mission is to bring the most up to date software and language teaching techniques to Thailand. We provide the ideal combination of international expertise and an in-depth understanding of Thai culture and learners’ needs. Our founding members have a special connection to Thailand, having lived here for many years and enjoyed the warmth and hospitality of the Thai people.

Team of Experts

Our tech team is made up of highly experienced programmers and designers. Our team of consultants is made up of some of the most experienced teachers, teacher trainers, examiners and writers working in the ELT industry today. Our consultants have an average of over 25 years’ industry experience and have written and delivered courses for organizations such as Cambridge English, International House, Trinity College London and the British Council.
