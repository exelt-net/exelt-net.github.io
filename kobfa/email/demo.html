Bring your interactive designs to life
Create higher-fidelity prototypes in Figma with two new prototyping features. You can now add multiple interactions to a single object to better reflect how users will engage with your designs. Plus, create custom eases to add dynamic animation and transitions to your prototypes.
Your top feature requests are live
Shadow spread: Adjust the positive or negative spread of your shadows to create depth, glows, and borders.

Letter spacing shortcuts: Fine-tune the letter spacing, line height, and font weight for individual letters, paragraphs, and selections with keyboard shortcuts.

Style picker improvements: Quickly browse through styles by scanning through a list view, or use the search bar to find the style you're looking for.
